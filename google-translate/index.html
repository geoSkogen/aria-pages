Undergraduate Programs

Show the world how different is done with a bachelor’s degree from Portland State University. Our undergraduate programs are nationally recognized for our hands-on approach to teaching and learning. Move forward on your four-year pathway to success with our unique University Studies program and University Honors College. Pick from more than 200 majors, minors, certificates and concentrations to find your perfect fit.

Online & Flexible Undergraduate Programs

Earn your bachelor’s degree where and when it’s convenient for you. Portland State University’s online and flexible programs work with your schedule. Our exceptional faculty and high academic standards make PSU’s accredited online programs a smart, affordable choice. You’ll do your coursework online or in person, earn a respected degree and achieve your career goals on your own schedule.

Graduate Programs

Unlock new solutions, expand your network and advance your career in graduate school at Portland State University. U.S. News & World Report recognizes our advanced degrees as among the best in the nation for our engaging courses that boost your earning potential, top faculty and groundbreaking research. Explore almost 90 master’s programs, 48 graduate certificates and 21 doctoral programs — and join us.

Online & Flexible Graduate Programs

Portland State University’s online and flexible graduate programs are student-focused and led by some of the nation’s best faculty and researchers. Take the next step in your career, but on your own time. You’ll gain the network, skills and experience you need to make progress in your life, career and community on your own schedule.
